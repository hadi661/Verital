QUALITY CONTROL DIVISION

The quality control division is a branch of the EPE/SPA VERITAL company based in Algiers. The missions of the quality control division are to provide services covering all services linked to national and international trade operators in accordance with laws, specifications and/or contractual forms. The quality control division is made up of multidisciplinary experts who can intervene at any time, both in Algeria and abroad with suppliers before shipping the goods.


PROCEDURES OPERATIONELLES


In their interventions, the experts use operational procedures established by the technical staff of the division, approved by the company's General Management and regularly verified by the quality assessors of the accrediting body, in particular:

Procedure to approve the product.
Procedure to monitor compliance.
Procedure inspect monitor loading, unloading and unloading.
Procedure for assessing damage and damage to products.
Procedure for taking samples for analysis.


MATERIALS AND MEANS

To respond quickly to our customers' requests and to respect their contractual requirements, the Quality Control Division is equipped with all the material resources necessary and useful for its activities.

INDUSTRY DIVISION

VERITAL INDUSTRIE, located on the five sites of Algiers, Oran, Annaba, Béjaïa and Hassi-Messaoud, provides technical assistance services for conformity control, expertise and calibration, intended to promote the safety of people and property . The services are carried out in accordance with the regulations in force and national and international standards in this area. Activities are carried out by a team of multidisciplinary experts, working in several fields both nationally and internationally.


PERIODIC REGULATORY SAFETY CONTROL

  • ELECTRICAL APPLIANCES (APE):

    Regulatory technical visit, inspection, earth measurements, insulation measurements, dielectric analysis, verification and testing of safety provisions.

  • GAS PRESSURE APPLIANCES (APG):

    Regulatory technical visit, ultrasound survey, endoscopy and non-destructive testing if necessary.

  • VAPOR PRESSURE DEVICES (APV):

    Regulatory technical visit, ultrasound survey, endoscopy and non-destructive testing if necessary.

  • LIFTING DEVICES (APL):

    Regulatory technical visit, inspection, static and dynamic tests, control and testing of safety devices.

RAIL AND GUIDED TRANSPORT DIVISION

As part of the missions devolved to the Ministry of Public Works and Transport for the issuance of operating authorizations for Guided Passenger Transport Systems, and in compliance with the regulated attributions applicable to approved qualified organizations (OQA), VERITAL is approved to carry out safety assessment missions for guided passenger transport. In its field of expertise, VERITAL provides in Algeria and abroad the provision and implementation of control, verification, expertise, analysis of potential problems, technical assistance and evaluation of the security of infrastructure of rail transport systems and guided.


TECHNICAL CONTROL OF RAIL AND GUIDED TRANSPORT IS BASED ON:

MONITORING GUIDED TRANSPORT FACILITIES
  • Conducting evidentiary examinations before putting STGs into public operation.
  • Audit and validation of all operations intended to verify the usability of STG installations in view of their reception.
  • Establishment and delivery of technical opinions for the operation of installations.

SUPERVISION AND MONITORING OF PROJECTS IN THE CONTEXT OF NEW INSTALLATIONS OR TECHNOLOGICAL UPGRADE OF EXISTING EQUIPMENT

CONTRIBUTION TO THE PROMOTION AND PRACTICE OF TECHNIQUES RELATING TO SAFETY IN GUIDED TRANSPORT FACILITIES
  • Technical control of STGs, their characteristics as well as the safety of their use or their implementation by all appropriate means such as visual inspection, sampling, laboratory examinations, tests and tests.
  • Overall safety assessment of STG.
  • examination of the design of plans, calculation notes and specifications of installations, materials, equipment, transport vehicles and specialized works.

    -monitoring of equipment, transport vehicles and specialized structures

    -monitoring of construction, manufacturing, repair and transformation operations of guided transport installations

ASSISTANCE AND ADVICE TO OPERATORS AND SERVICES OF THE MINISTRY OF TRANSPORT DTTU (DIRECTION OF LAND AND URBAN TRANSPORT)

CONDUCTING TECHNICAL EXPERTISE ON RAIL AND GUIDED TRANSPORT FACILITIES
